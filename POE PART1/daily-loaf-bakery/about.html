Our Story

From a small home kitchen to your neighborhood bakery

Baking Since 2020

The Daily Loaf was born during the pandemic when founder Sarah Mitchell started baking sourdough for her neighbors. What began as a weekly hobby quickly turned into a community obsession.

By 2022, we opened our first storefront in Cape Town's vibrant Woodstock neighborhood. Today, we serve over 500 customers daily and supply fresh bread to local cafes and restaurants.

We believe that good bread takes time. Our sourdough undergoes a 24-hour fermentation process, developing complex flavors and making it easier to digest.

Our Values
